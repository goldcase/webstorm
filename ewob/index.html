WHO WE ARE

ABOUT

EWOB is a collaborative effort between faculty, staff and students founded at the University of Illinois. We overlay pioneering research, teaching, and social initiatives on subsistence marketplaces.

MISSION

EWOB focuses on enabling entrepreneurship in impoverished communities around the world. By doing this, we develop students for the global challenges of the 21st century.

VISION

By applying decades of academic research, EWOB solves problems in small communities lacking in marketplace and entrepreneurial literacy.
